Alien Rampage is an endless runner with detailed 2D physics and cartoon gore.

You play as an alien that has been captured and experimented upon by a nefarious non-government organisation. When a virus causes the facility to be overrun by zombies you are released to clear a path.

It's pandemonium.

- 2D Ragdoll physics, particles and gore.
- 9 Weapons.
- 4 Enemy types.
- 6 Power-up types.
- Procedurally generated terrain.
